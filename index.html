About Me

I am an AI Safety Researcher focusing on ensuring the responsible development of artificial intelligence. My research interests include AI alignment, safety standards, and policy. I work on developing frameworks and methodologies to help mitigate risks from advanced AI systems.

Projects

AI Standards Lab

Co-leading an initiative focused on developing standards and best practices for the safe deployment of AI systems. Our work aims to bridge technical research with practical implementation guidelines.

Discernment

A research project exploring how AI systems can better identify and respond to potentially harmful instructions. This work focuses on enhancing AI safety through improved decision-making capabilities.

Short Timelines Thinking

Research on forecasting and preparation strategies for potentially rapid developments in artificial general intelligence, with emphasis on governance and technical safeguards.
